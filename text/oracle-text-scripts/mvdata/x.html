Some details about the pre-season trackday at Donington on Sunday 13th March:

Ethos

This is a track day, not a test day.  It is designed as a social occasion for our drivers and their families and supporters to meet up and have some fun whilst "blowing out the cobwebs". There will be novice drivers and passengers on track and aggressive driving will not be tolerated.

Organisers

The day is being organised for CGRC by Bookatrack.  Please refer to their terms and conditions. We will be running open pitlane except for two novice sessions.

Please note that Bookatrack normally offer a "no queues" guarantee.  Since the number of bookings was set by GCRC, not Bookatrack, that guarantee does not apply for this event.  The amount of queing (if any) is likely to depend on the number of extra drivers going out. We do want to encourage friends and relatives go out, but please don't abuse the system.

Sign On and Briefing

Will be in the Paddock Suite (large building to the left of the garages).

DON'T FORGET to bring your road driving licence. An MSA race licence is not an acceptable substitute. Foreign licences should be acceptable if you don't hold a UK licence.

There is no scrutineering, but our series scrutineer Keith Marchment will be attending, and will be happy to look over your car or answer any questions about preparedness for racing.

Late Briefings

All drivers must attend a briefing. Any drivers not able to attend the morning briefing will have to speak nicely to Bookatrack and ask for a personal briefing.

Lunch

Bookatrack are organising a hog roast for us in their new "Caterham Midlands" facility on the edge of the main paddock. There should be enough to go round if each person brings the usual one guest. Please don't take the mickey - if you've got five friends turning up please ask them to use the Paddock Diner instead.

Tea and coffee will be available all day in garage 17/18.

Paddock Access Saturday

To be determined, watch the forum for more information.  Worst case you will be able to park outside the paddock entrance overnight, but we hope to do better than that.
